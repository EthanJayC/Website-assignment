Mission: Artemis 2024
Agency: NASA
Launch date: c. 2024
Overview:
Landing at the Moon south pole, the Artemis program has been designed with the objective of preparing NASA to take Astronauts to Mars by using the Moon as a "battle ground" to develop advanced engineering techniques with habitation and to measure the effect long durations of space travel has on Humans.
